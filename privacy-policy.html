Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2025

This Chrome extension, MN Pay Transparency Reporter, does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.

What this extension does:

  • Reads the URL of the current browser tab (with user interaction)
  • Uses that URL to open a pre-filled email draft in Gmail
  • Performs all operations locally in your browser

What this extension does NOT do:

  • It does NOT collect or store any personally identifiable information
  • It does NOT track your browsing activity
  • It does NOT connect to any external servers or APIs
  • It does NOT use analytics or third-party cookies
